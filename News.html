Industrial Visit

An Industrial Visit to the region of Manali – Chandigarh – Amritsar was jointly organised by the CSI and FACE committee between 5th January 2012 and 14th January 2012. 141 students joined the trip along with some faculty members. The students visited the HMT factory in Panchkula in Chandigarh. They also visited various tourist destinations. They established new bonds of friendship and got much needed rejuvenation after a tiring semester.

Give people Life

Blood Donation Drive

The CSI-S.P.I.T, student branch organized a Blood Donation Drive with the NGO, 'Think Foundation'. We got tremendous response from students and faculty. The doctors and supporting staff arrived at 10.30 am. Beds and other required equipments were arranged in the room.

More than 200 students came for the noble cause. Blood tests were conducted by doctors. Students having low haemoglobin count were not allowed to donate blood. Every donor received a certificate and a donor card. The donor card enables each donor or his family members to get free 1 unit of blood if required from J J Hospital. The donor card is valid for 2 yrs. By the end of the day, 143 units of blood were collected. The drive was successful in terms of units of blood collected and in spreading awareness about the importance of blood donation.
